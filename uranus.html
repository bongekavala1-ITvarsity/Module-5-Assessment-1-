Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun.Its not visible to the naked eye.

Uranus is he first planet discovered with the use of a telescope.Uranus is tipped over on its side with an axial tilt of 98 degrees.It is often described as "rolling around the Sun on its side."
